About Me

I'm Chemical Engineer by profession but, Software Developer by passion!!

This is my story ...

Hello there!!, I was born in Villavicencio, Colombia, but grew up living in the cities Manizales and Medellín. My curiosity to understand how the world works led me to study chemical engineering, a career that taught me to view things from multiple perspectives.

For over 8 years, I worked as a chemical engineer, specializing in water treatment and environmental technologies. This period gave me experience in leadership, project management, and complex problem-solving. I also ventured into various entrepreneurial projects, from craft brewing to environmental consulting. It was in the midst of these projects that I had my first encounters with programming. I used digital tools to solve problems in my work, which sparked my interest in technology.

The coronavirus pandemic marked a turning point in my life. Amidst the uncertainty, I secured a scholarship offered by Globant and MercadoLibre to study software development. This opportunity allowed me to change course and immerse myself in the world of programming. Since then, I've worked on various development projects, from local startups to my current position as a full stack developer for an international company.

This path has allowed me to combine my engineering experience with my new technology skills. Today, I find myself at an exciting point in my career, integrating my analytical engineering background with the creativity of software development.

-- I'm ready to face new challenges and continue growing in the field of technology.
